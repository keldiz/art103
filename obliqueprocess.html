Artist Statement
This is one of my favorite projects because I've never used a 3D modeling software or Unity until last year.
After taking two Art courses that introduced 3D and game designs, I developed an interest in creating assets.
I am proud of this work because I worked with a group on this game. We faced many challenges.
Working with a group of students is always challenging because we have differnet workloads
and more often than not, we are behind on schedule. However, for this project we decided that
we're gonna give it our best and produce the best game we've ever made. The project was very successful
and we surprised a lot of students including our professor.
